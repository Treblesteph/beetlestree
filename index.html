Information

The beetles tree was developed by Steph Smith-Unna using Materialize and D3, and is based on this great interactive tree template. The colour-scheme was heavily influenced by the chic-mellow scheme in PLTTS.me.

The higher level taxonomic organisation follows that of Bouchard et al., 2011.

Detailed images of European Chrysomeloid beetles were taken with permission from Lech Borowiec's comprehensive website, and images of many British specimens were taken from the Watford Coleoptera Group website.
